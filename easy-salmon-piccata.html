Easy Salmon Piccata

Description

a lightened up, quick and easy seared salmon piccata with an authentic piccata sauce of lemon, garlic and capers, and the optional white wine. Anything Piccata sounds so refined and way too complicated to cook, but this Salmon Piccata recipe is easy, gourmet cooking in minutes!

With this impressive Piccata, we become humble home cooks that can almost pass for 5-star chefs in our own tiny kitchens with screaming children or ear-piercing loud televisions in the background. No one would know with a plate full of this in front of them.
